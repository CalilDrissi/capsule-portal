Running Capsule as the platform admin

The admin account owns the platform — you use it to set up firms and keep an eye on things. Firms and their accountants do the day-to-day work. Follow these steps in order; we'll add each one here as you go.

02

Create your firm

Do this now
  1. In the left sidebar, click Firms (top icon).
  2. Click New firm (top-right).
  3. Enter your Firm name and the Accountant full name. Leave username blank to auto-generate; the password is pre-filled (you can edit it).
  4. Click Create firm.
  5. Copy the accountant username and password shown — that's the firm's day-to-day login. It only appears once.

A firm is one accounting practice, fully isolated from every other firm. The accountant login you just created is what you (or the firm) use to add clients and review documents — not the admin login.

03

Sign in as the accountant & invite a client

Do this now
  1. Top-right, click the log out icon to leave the admin account.
  2. Sign in with the accountant username and password from Step 2.
  3. You land on Clients. Click New client (top-right).
  4. Enter the client's Display name (required) and click Create.
  5. Copy the invite link shown and send it to your client (email, message — however you like).

The accountant account is what you'll use day to day — adding clients, reviewing their documents, and requesting missing paperwork. The admin account is only for creating firms.
